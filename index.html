A little bit about me...

I graduated from Wade College with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Merchandising & Design in 2015. After I continued my retail career for 5 more years working as a visual merchandiser and store manager. Growing tired of retail and wanted to get back into a more creative role I started looking for a new career path. Wanting to continue to help people through experience I found UX and UI design. Enrolling in the bootcamp at UT Austin in July of 2020 I was challenged but developed new skills and knowledge in the following 6 months.
Now I am ready to help people through design by creating impactful and enjoyable experiences. I have new skills in user research, prototyping (Figma, Adobe XD), user testing and more.
